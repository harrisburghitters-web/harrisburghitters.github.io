Registration Info
Registration Fee $150 per player
No-Refund Policy All registration fees are non-refundable once paid. Please confirm eligibility and commitment before submitting registration and payment.
Age Cutoff Rule Age groups are determined by the player’s age as of June 1 of the current season year. Example: If a player turns 13 on June 1, 2025, they play 12U for the 2025 season.
Practice & Playing Time Attendance matters. Players earn playing time through practice participation, effort, and readiness. Athletes who miss practices should expect reduced playing time during games.
