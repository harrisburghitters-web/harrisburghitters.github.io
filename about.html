About Us

The Harrisburg Hitters are a youth travel football program committed to development, discipline, and a team-first culture.

Build confident athletes through consistent coaching, accountability, and competitive opportunities—on and off the field.

Vision

Develop a nationally respected program that prepares athletes for the next level by mastering fundamentals, discipline, and leadership.
