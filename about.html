About Us

The Harrisburg Hitters are a youth football organization serving children and families in and around Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. Our program is designed to support the healthy development of youth through structured athletics, mentorship, character building, and positive community engagement.

Our Charitable Purpose

Harrisburg Hitters Youth Football Association exists for charitable and educational purposes. We aim to improve the lives of youth by providing a safe, structured environment that promotes leadership, accountability, and teamwork while encouraging academic success and positive decision-making. Through coaching, mentorship, and community involvement, we work to:

  • Support academics by reinforcing the importance of school performance, attendance, and personal responsibility.
  • Reduce youth trouble by keeping participants engaged in supervised, constructive activities and positive peer groups.
  • Provide mentorship through trained coaches and volunteers who model discipline, respect, and leadership.
  • Promote health and wellness through fitness, fundamentals, and age-appropriate athletic training.
  • Strengthen community by involving families, encouraging volunteerism, and creating opportunities for service and outreach.
Mission

Provide youth in and around Harrisburg with coaching, mentorship, and competitive opportunities that build character, discipline, teamwork, and academic-minded habits—on and off the field.

Vision

Create a nationally respected youth football program and nonprofit community resource that develops confident student-athletes, prepares them for future success, and contributes to safer neighborhoods through positive youth development.

Harrisburg Hitters Youth Football Association

Harrisburg Hitters Youth Football Association is our nonprofit organization focused on charitable and educational youth development in the Harrisburg, PA area. We are committed to transparency, responsible stewardship, and community impact through athletics, mentorship, and family engagement.
